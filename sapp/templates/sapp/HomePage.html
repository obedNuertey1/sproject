Computer Engineering Digital Collections(CPENspace)

CPENspace is the institutional repository of the Department of computer Engineering, School of Engineering Sciences. This space is for the collection, preservation and distribution of digital materials. CPENspace was established to facilitate the store and manage the digital content of projects and other scholary contents of undergraduate students studying Computer Engineering
